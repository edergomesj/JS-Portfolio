Hi there! I’m a data analyst with experience in turning complex datasets into meaningful insights. With a track record of delivering data-driven solutions for major financial institutions, government agencies, and prominent companies in Brazil, my expertise spans data extraction, transformation, and in-depth analysis to create impactful visualizations, reports, and predictive models.

I’m passionate about exploring data and uncovering patterns that others might overlook. Exploring data isn’t just my job; it’s a passion. I love diving into new datasets and uncovering patterns that others might overlook. My curiosity keeps me motivated to continually learn and stay up-to-date with industry advancements. Known for being detail-oriented, I believe that precision and accuracy are essential in making data truly valuable. I’m also a strong team player who enjoys collaborating with others to drive results and build solutions together.
